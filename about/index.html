About

What is t0.network?

t0 is an early-stage Open Source software consultancy firm. We help companies to understand how Open Source software works, why it is or might not be a fit for them, help them with the particulars of creating vibrant and self-sustained projects.

We also offer coaching and supervisioning for companies growing through the process of opening up the source of a project, avoid credential leaks, possible security problems and helping them with the legalese involved in Open Source.

We do not develop new projects, and do not take long contracts, but we can help pointing other companies that do that work if necessary.
