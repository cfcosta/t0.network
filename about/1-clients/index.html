What do you do?

In summary, we help companies interact with the Open Source world, providing guidance to create communities around products, understand the benefits and drawbacks when working with Open Source, and help companies financially sponsor development of projects.

Assuming you’re the Founder/CEO/CTO of a company that is interested in exploring Open Source as an option for one or more products, here’s what we can help you with:

  • Choose a License
  • Create the projects on the correct places, and set up the tools needed (CI, Lints)
  • Choose communication tools (IM, emails, project communications)
  • Choose development and management tools
  • Check for possible leak of credentials and remove them from VCS history
  • Choose a Code of Conduct
  • Detail policies for interactions between the core team and contributors
  • Define processes and ideas to bring community contributors

We can also help you with adopting existing Open Source projects for in-house maintenance or to sponsor development for the original core team.
