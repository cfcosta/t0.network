What is Tier0?

Tier0 is two things: an Open Source software company, and an Open Source Software Collective, both with a common goal to maintain and promote Open Source as a viable approach to software development.

To do so, we do two things:

  • Support some selected Open Source projects, with both development time and money.
  • Provide enterprise-level support and development for those projects.
